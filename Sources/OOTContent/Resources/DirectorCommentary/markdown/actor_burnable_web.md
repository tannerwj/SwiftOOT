The Deku Tree's webs are actors because progression gates in OoT usually need **logic plus collision**, not just art.

- A web barrier can block movement, test for fire interactions, and then tear itself out of the scene once the condition is met.
- Making that an actor keeps the rule local instead of hardcoding one-off dungeon checks in the room loader.
- It is a clean example of why even scenery in OoT is often gameplay code wearing an environmental costume.
