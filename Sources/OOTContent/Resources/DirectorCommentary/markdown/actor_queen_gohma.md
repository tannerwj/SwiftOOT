Queen Gohma reads like a boss fight storyboard translated into code.

- Each phase controls movement, eye exposure, attack timing, and when the boss is vulnerable.
- The script can also spawn larva helpers, which keeps pressure on the player even when the main boss is between attack beats.
- This is one of OoT's early examples of a boss actor owning both spectacle and encounter pacing instead of delegating everything to cutscene code.
